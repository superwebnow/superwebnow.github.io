Who is Farid Rueda?

Farid Rueda, a Mexican street artist known for painting graffiti full of cultural references of his country. The way he describes these issues is not what you would expect: Rueda discovers new concepts to represent Mexican popular culture without turning it into a cliché.

Where was Farid Rueda born?

Rueda was born in Morelos, officially titled as a Free and Sovereign State of Morelos. Mexican pride runs deep in these lands, so it’s not surprising to see how much affection this artist has for his country. When young Farid turned the age of nine, his family moved to Xochimilco, México City.
