Lauren James Biography

Lauren James is one of the most popular and richest Association Football Player who was born on September 29, 2001 in England, United Kingdom. Lauren James (born 29 September 2001) is an English footballer who plays forward for the FA WSL team Manchester United.

In January of 2019, James received her first invitation to the squad for under-19s to play in the Algarve Tournament. In July, James was named in the England team to participate in the 2019 UEFA Under-19 Championship in Scotland.

On the 13th of July, 2018 it was announced that James was to make it into the first professionally-trained Manchester United squad due to take part in the new restructured the 2018-19 FA Women’s Championship. She made her first competitive appearance in the team of Manchester United in a 1-0 League Cup victory against Liverpool on 19 August. She scored the first two goals in the United’s opening fixture of the new 2018-19 Championship season with a 12-0 loss against Aston Villa. James was named FA Women’s Championship player of the month in September, following her scoring three goals in Manchester United’s streak of unbeaten play in the opening month. On the 20th of April, 2019 James had four goals scored in the 7-0 win over Crystal Palace.
