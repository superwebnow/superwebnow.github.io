Eric Carter Biography

Eric Carter is one of the most popular and richest Canadian Football Player who was born on January 23, 1969 in United States. Eric Carter (born January 23, 1969 in Jesup, Georgia) was a football player in the Canadian Football League for eleven seasons. Carter played as a cornerback for the Hamilton Tiger-Cats from 1994-1998, the BC Lions from 1999-2003 and the Winnipeg Blue Bombers in 2004. He was a CFL All-Star four times and won a Grey Cup for the Lions in 2000. He played college football at Knoxville College.
