Eleanor Parker Biography

Eleanor Parker is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on June 26, 1922 in Cedarville, Ohio, United States. Versatile actress who has played comic, serious, and romantic roles in films such as Scaramouche. She received widespread recognition for her role as Baroness Elsa Schrader in the hit 1967 musical The Sound of Music.
