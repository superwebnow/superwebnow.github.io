Tony Orlando Biography

Tony Orlando is one of the most popular and richest Pop Singer who was born on April 3, 1944 in New York City, New York State, United States. Pop singer who recorded “Knock Three Times” with backup singer Dawn. He also released hits like “Bless You” and “Halfway to Paradise.”

He had a small role in Waking up in Reno, which starred Patrick Swayze.

He married Elaine Orlando in 1965; after their divorce in 1984, he married Francine Amormino in 1991. He has a daughter named Jenny and a son named Jon.
